## 📞 Q&A Preparation

### **Expected Questions & Answers:**

**Q: How is this different from ChatGPT?**
A: ChatGPT is general-purpose. We're specialized for Nigerian commerce - we understand Pidgin, integrate with local payments, know Nigerian locations and business context. Plus we're built into WhatsApp where customers already are.

**Q: What if Meta changes their WhatsApp API?**
A: We're using the official Meta Cloud API for businesses. It's their revenue stream, so they actively support it. We also have contingency plans for SMS and other channels.

**Q: How do you handle AI hallucinations?**
A: We use a hybrid approach - AI for language understanding, deterministic logic for critical operations like pricing and orders. Product info comes from actual database, not AI imagination.

**Q: Customer acquisition cost?**
A: Estimated ₦5,000-₦8,000 CAC. With ₦15,000 average monthly price, payback period is < 1 month. LTV:CAC ratio of 10:1+.

**Q: Competition from international players?**
A: We have first-mover advantage and cultural context they can't replicate quickly. By the time they localize, we'll have market dominance and network effects.

**Q: Why not target other African countries?**
A: Nigeria first to dominate one market. But our tech is ready - expansion to Ghana, Kenya, South Africa is roadmap for Year 2.

**Q: Technical challenges?**
A: WhatsApp rate limits - solved with queue system. AI costs - negotiated volume discounts + local LLaMA model. Scale - AWS auto-scaling ready.

**Q: Exit strategy?**
A: Acquisition targets: Paystack, Flutterwave, Jumia, international WhatsApp business platforms. Or IPO in 5-7 years. Market validates $100M+ valuations for similar platforms.
