- **Proactive service**: Immediately finds order without asking for number

## 🎬 Demo Presentation Tips

### Setup (2 minutes before)
1. Open WhatsApp Demo page on laptop (mirrored to projector)
2. Have these scenarios ready in notes
3. Test audio if playing message sounds
4. Keep phone nearby for "real WhatsApp" demonstration

### Flow for Each Scenario (30-45 seconds)
1. **Introduce the business type**: "This is Fashion Paradise, a women's clothing store in Lagos"
2. **Set the scene**: "Watch how a customer discovers and orders a product"
3. **Type/click through conversation**: Let investors see the natural flow
4. **Highlight key moment**: "Notice how the AI detected purchase intent and confirmed the order"
5. **Show result**: "Order created, payment link sent, all automated"

### Key Points to Emphasize
- ⚡ **Speed**: "Response in 2-3 seconds"
- 💰 **Cost Savings**: "₦384,000/month saved vs hiring 2 agents"
- 🌍 **Scale**: "One AI handles 1000+ conversations simultaneously"
- 🇳🇬 **Local**: "Speaks Nigerian Pidgin, understands local context"
- 🎯 **Conversion**: "40%+ of conversations result in orders"
- 📈 **24/7**: "Never sleeps, never sick, always professional"
