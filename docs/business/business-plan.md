# WazAssist AI - Business Plan

**Document Version**: 1.0
**Date**: December 24, 2025
**Market**: Nigerian SMEs
**Business Model**: SaaS + Educational Content

---

## Executive Summary

WazAssist AI is an AI-powered WhatsApp Business Assistant designed specifically for Nigerian Small and Medium Enterprises (SMEs). The platform automates customer interactions, processes orders, generates invoices, and provides 24/7 support in multiple Nigerian languages including English, Pidgin, Yoruba, Igbo, and Hausa.

**Dual Revenue Model**:
1. **SaaS Subscriptions**: Monthly recurring revenue from businesses
2. **Educational Content**: Teaching developers how to build similar systems

**Year 1 Target**: ₦217,900,000 (~$272,000) in revenue with 86%+ profit margins

---

## Market Analysis

### Target Market: Nigerian SMEs

**Market Size**:
- 41.5 million MSMEs in Nigeria (SMEDAN 2021)
- Addressable market: ~5 million SMEs with smartphones
- Target segment: 2 million SMEs using WhatsApp Business

**Market Problems**:
1. **Limited Service Hours**: Most SMEs operate 8-10 hours/day, missing customer inquiries
2. **Language Barriers**: Need to communicate in local languages
3. **Manual Processes**: Order tracking, invoicing done manually
4. **Payment Collection**: Difficulty tracking payments
5. **No Analytics**: Limited insights into customer behavior

**Our Solution Value**:
- 24/7 automated customer service
- Multi-language support (5 Nigerian languages)
- Automated order processing & invoicing
- Integrated payment collection (Paystack/Flutterwave)
- Business analytics dashboard

### Competitive Landscape

**Direct Competitors**:
1. **Wati.io** - Global WhatsApp platform (₦15k-50k/month)
   - Weakness: No Nigerian language support, expensive
2. **Respond.io** - Multi-channel messaging (₦20k-60k/month)
   - Weakness: Not Nigeria-optimized, complex setup
3. **JungleWorks** - WhatsApp commerce (₦25k-75k/month)
   - Weakness: High cost, no local payment integration

**Competitive Advantages**:
1. ✅ **Nigerian-First Design**: Pidgin, Yoruba, Igbo, Hausa support
2. ✅ **Lower Cost**: Starting at ₦5k/month (vs ₦15k+ competitors)
3. ✅ **Local Payment Integration**: Paystack & Flutterwave built-in
4. ✅ **AI-Powered**: Advanced LLaMA 3.1 models
5. ✅ **Easy Setup**: 5-minute onboarding vs hours for competitors
6. ✅ **Local Support**: Nigeria-based support team

## Risks & Mitigation

### Technical Risks

**Risk 1**: AWS cost overruns
- **Mitigation**: Strict monitoring, auto-scaling limits, cost alerts

**Risk 2**: AI accuracy issues
- **Mitigation**: Human fallback, continuous training, user feedback loops

**Risk 3**: WhatsApp API changes
- **Mitigation**: Stay updated with Meta, use official API, have backup (360Dialog)

### Business Risks

**Risk 1**: Low customer adoption
- **Mitigation**: Beta testing, competitive pricing, strong marketing

**Risk 2**: High churn
- **Mitigation**: Excellent support, regular feature updates, customer success team

**Risk 3**: Competitor copying
- **Mitigation**: Fast execution, customer relationships, continuous innovation

### Regulatory Risks

**Risk 1**: Data protection (NDPR)
- **Mitigation**: Compliance from day 1, encryption, data residency

**Risk 2**: Payment regulations
- **Mitigation**: Use licensed partners (Paystack/Flutterwave)
